At SMU, we place strong emphasis on
developing our graduates to
be global-ready citizens.

Through our various global exposure programmes,
you have opportunities to gain new perspectives to
important societal and global issues, work in
multi-cultural teams, and cultivate an appreciation of the
interconnectedness of world communities.

Come explore the wide array of global opportunities at
Study Abroad Fair 2022.

What You Can Expect

“Live” Talks

Hear from over 40 partners in XX countries on their short-term and semester-long exchange programmes.

Emerging Opportunities in ASEAN, China and India (ACI)

Explore overseas learning opportunities in the ACI region to propel yourself into the drivers of Asian success.

Peer-to-Peer Sharing Sessions

Chat with your peers who are currently abroad for exchange and interact with the incoming exchange students to learn more about their home countries.
